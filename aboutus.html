about us.

Founded in 2025, Art Adventure was born from the desire to give people a place to slow down, reconnect, and experience creativity in its most joyful form. Our studio is built around one simple idea: when people create together, they connect on a deeper, more meaningful level. Here, families share uninterrupted moments, friends unwind and laugh, and colleagues discover a fresh way to bond. Every workshop is designed to be calming, hands-on, and welcoming to all ages—no pressure, no expectations, just the freedom to explore and enjoy the process of making art.

At the heart of Art Adventure are our instructors, whose warmth and passion shape every experience:

Meet our instructors

Lina, our lead workshop guide, is known for her gentle teaching style and her love for color and composition. With years of experience working with children and mixed-age groups, she creates an atmosphere where everyone feels confident, supported, and free to express themselves.

Mark, our mosaic and craft specialist, brings a thoughtful, patient approach to every session. He believes creativity thrives when people feel at ease, and he has a talent for helping guests transform simple materials into pieces filled with personality and meaning.

Together, they cultivate a space where creativity feels natural, where conversations flow easily, and where every guest leaves not just with artwork, but with a memory worth keeping.
Art Adventure is more than a studio—it's a place to laugh, to connect, and to be inspired. It's a place to create moments that last.
